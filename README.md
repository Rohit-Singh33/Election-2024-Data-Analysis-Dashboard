#  India General Election Results Dashboard 2024 (Power BI)

## Overview
This Power BI dashboard visualizes the **2024 Indian General Election Results** with clear insights into national and state-level outcomes.  
It provides an interactive breakdown of seats by alliance, party, and candidate.


##  Features
- National-level summary of seats (NDA, I.N.D.I.A, and Others)
- State-wise detailed results with charts and filters
- Party-wise and alliance-wise breakdown
- Dynamic visuals for easier comparison

- ##  Tools Used
- **Power BI Desktop**
- **Excel / CSV Data Source**
- **DAX Measures** for calculations
- **Custom Visuals** for pie and card charts

## 1  National-Level Insights

NDA Alliance Leads Overall

NDA secured 292 seats (54%) out of 543 total seats.

This indicates a clear majority and strong performance at the national level.

I.N.D.I.A Alliance Gained Significant Ground

I.N.D.I.A Alliance won 234 seats (43%), showing a major improvement compared to previous elections — reflecting rising opposition strength.

Other Parties Hold a Minor Share

Smaller parties captured only 17 seats (3%), showing a two-front competition between NDA and I.N.D.I.A alliances.

Dominant Party in NDA

BJP alone secured 240 seats, making up the bulk of NDA’s total — a clear indicator of BJP’s continued national dominance.

Strong Regional Support for Allies

TDP (16), JD(U) (12), and SHS (7) played crucial roles in strengthening the NDA coalition.

## 2️. I.N.D.I.A Alliance Insights

INC and SP Show Strong Comeback

Indian National Congress (INC) won 99 seats, showing its revival in several states.

Samajwadi Party (SP) emerged as a key regional player with 37 seats.

Regional Parties Increase Representation

Multiple smaller parties like DMK, AITC, and RJD contributed meaningfully, making the I.N.D.I.A alliance diverse and geographically widespread.

 ## 3️ State-Level (Uttar Pradesh) Insights

SP Dominates in Uttar Pradesh

SP secured 37 out of 80 seats (46.25%), becoming the largest party in the state.

This shows a strong voter shift toward the I.N.D.I.A alliance in UP.

BJP Maintains Strong Base

BJP retained 33 seats (41.25%), showing continued but slightly reduced influence compared to earlier elections.

INC and RLD Minor Gains

INC (6 seats) and RLD (2 seats) also gained representation, showing that the I.N.D.I.A alliance’s teamwork paid off in multiple constituencies.

Highly Competitive Political Landscape

The small difference between I.N.D.I.A (43 seats) and NDA (36 seats) reflects a tightly contested state election, marking UP as the key battleground for future elections.

 ## 4️ Visual & Analytical Insights (from Dashboard View)
